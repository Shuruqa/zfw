Zero-Food-Waste (ZFW) Management Platform

A platform that furthers the movement of “Zero-Food-Waste”
by assisting restaurants with redistributing food waste through
proper recycling processes and fair food distribution.

The Problem

Why is food waste detrimental?

Food waste is a societal and humanitarian issue but most importantly an environmental issue.
This is due to CO2 emissions for food facilitation and methane emissions from due the waste filling landfills.
This adds to the food insecurities faced throughout the world seen with the U.S. alone wasting $218 billion dollars.

01

Confusing Labels

Consumers waste a significant amount of food due to the confusion that's faced when attempting to understand food lables such as best-by, use-by, sell-by dates commonly seen.
02

Oversized Servings & Containers

Commonly seen in the U.S., the oversizing of food products results in food waste as consumers are incapable of consuming the amounts of food purchased.
03

Consumer Behaviours

This can include overpurchasing of foods, looking for the "perfect produce", and poor food perparation practices.
04

Inadequate Technologies

There is a lack of technologies applied today that can assist in reducing food waste. This can include proper food waste tracking systems for grocery stores or even proper storage technologies.

05

Lack of Food Rescue/Recycling Services

Services that provide consumers and restaurants the opportunity to donate or properly recycle food is very limited causing accessibility issues. This results in a lack of action from consuming parties.

Our Impact

How Are We Making
Making An Impact?

Our team has developed the Zero-Food-Waste Management Platform in order to connect various users types and enable them to connect and redistribute food whether through donations for those in need or proper food waste recycling.

Once the system is fully implemented, functioning, and takes on data, it will help to make data-driven insights into food waste patterns, collect and analyze data from both donors and receivers, and potentially enable businesses to optimize their inventory management and reduce waste further.

Establishing a Connection

The most important aspect of our platform is that it enables various users to connect in order to redistribute food.

Simple User Interface

The user interface and functionalities of this platform are simple but efficient and enable users to easily understand and access various components of ZFW

Easy Pick-Up Requests

Food Businesses can easily submit food for availability and the selections made by Food Waste Facilitators or Food Requesters, those looking for donated food, are easily communicated throughout the platform.
