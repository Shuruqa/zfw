Our Initiative

Tackling Food Waste,
One Business at a Time

Zero-Food-Waste (ZFW) is a small environment-oriented start-up established to focus on the reduction of food-waste and reduce environmental pollution that stems from the massive amounts of food that are thrown away.

To reach this goal we developed a platform to assist in facilitating waste management and empower restuarants and other industries in Washington, D.C. in hopes address the detrimental impact food waste has on our planet.
